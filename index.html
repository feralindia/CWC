    • Hydrologic and Carbon Services in the Western Ghats: Response of Forests and Agro-ecosystems to Extreme Rainfall Events

    R scripts to process rain gauge data for the CWC project.

    Project maintained by feralindia Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    The scripts presented here were created as part of a project supported by the Ministry of Earth Sciences, Govt. of India and the Natural Environment Research Council - UK, under the Changing Water Cycle (CWC) programme. The official UK web site for the project is https://www.bgs.ac.uk/changingwatercycle/WesternGhats.html.

    Various authors have contributed to the scripts, all of which are released under the GPL. Please note that the script itself calls libraries and programmes which may be released under different licensing terms.
